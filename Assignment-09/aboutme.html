About Me


Hi, I was born in Butte, Montana, but grew up in Helena, Montana. As an adult, I moved around a bit and have lived in Seattle and Spokane, Washington as well as Northern Idaho. Eventually though, I missed home and came back and settled in Butte, Montana. I currently reside in Butte with my boyfriend Nicholas and our dog Messi, although we are building a house in Anaconda, Montana where Nick runs his two family pharmacies.

In my free time, I love to snuggle with my dog, listen to true crime podcasts, or binge watch tv. I am passionate about a lot of issues and also spend my time doing historical research on subjects that interest me. I am very close with my two sisters and many nieces and nephews.
