The Experiment

This is an experiment. I am going to write a short story. It is not a particularly interesting story, but it is something which fills up some space. Please feel free to skip because there is nothing of great importance included.

It was a hot and smoky day in early September. My next door neighbors had decided to have a garage sale. While I support their decision to purge their unneccessary household items, I don't particularly like a bunch of strangers leering at me while I take my dog outside.

Like the socially anxious person I am, I decided the best course of action was to hide on the side of of my garage... as any self respecting adult does. Unfortunately, I found out the hard way that strangers leering at me is preferrable to what happened while I hid. As I cowered a big hairy spider dropped from the eaves and fell into my hair. Not only did I make my presence known to the bargain shoppers, but I screamed so fearfully, that the whole neighborhood probably worried a crime was being committed. That was the day that I learned sometimes hiding from things you dislike may result in a spider falling into your hair.
