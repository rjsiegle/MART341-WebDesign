Robyn Siegle - Media Artist



Robyn Siegle is proficient in Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe After Effects, as well as Procreate. In her free time she enjoys painting using acrylics, drawing using charcoal or colored pencils, and has a variety of hobbies including hand stenciling furniture.
