About the Artist:

Robyn was born in Butte but grew up in Helena, Montana. Robyn graduated from Capital High School in 2004. As an adult, Robyn has lived in Washington (Seattle and Spokane) as well as Northern Idaho. However, she has strong ties to Montana and has settled in Butte, Montana. She currently lives in Butte with her boyfriend Nicholas and dog Messi. Eventually the couple plans to move to Anaconda where Nick runs his two family pharmacies.

In her free time, Robyn loves to snuggle with her dog, listen to true crime podcasts, and binge watch TV shows. Robyn is passionate about many issues and spends her free time doing historical research on subjects of interest. She is also close with her sisters and many nieces and nephews. In May she will be helping her nephew paint a mural at his school
